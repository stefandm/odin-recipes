No description for outsiders.Too much information is no good, amore.Familia secrets.

Ingredients

  • penne
  • pepper
  • pecorino
  • eggs
  • guanciale

Steps

  1. boil pasta to al dente
  2. fry guanciale on low-med heat
  3. in separate bowl mix eggs with pecorino
  4. add pasta to guanciale pan
  5. remove from heat
  6. add the eggs & pecorino
  7. mix for 2 minutes
  8. add pepper
  9. scream mamma mia
